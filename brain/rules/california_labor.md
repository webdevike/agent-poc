# California Labor Rules

## Final Pay Requirements
- **Involuntary termination**: Final wages due IMMEDIATELY on last day.
- **Voluntary resignation with 72+ hours notice**: Final wages due on last day.
- **Voluntary resignation without 72 hours notice**: Final wages due within 72 hours.
- Includes all accrued, unused PTO (California treats PTO as earned wages).

## Penalties for Late Final Pay
- Waiting time penalty: one day's wages for each day late, up to 30 days.
- This is a significant financial risk — always flag if `final_pay_date` is after `last_work_date`.

## WARN Act (California)
- California WARN is stricter than federal.
- Applies to establishments with 75+ employees (federal is 100+).
- Covers layoffs of 50+ employees in a 30-day period.
- Requires 60 days advance notice.
